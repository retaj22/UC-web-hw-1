X or O

The game is to be played between two people (in this program between HUMAN and COMPUTER).
One of the player chooses (O) and the other (X) to mark their respective cells.
The game starts with one of the players and the game ends when one of the players
has one whole row/ column/ diagonal filled with his/her respective character (‘O’ or ‘X’).
If no one wins, then the game is said to be draw.

  1. Two players
  2. One player is O and the other is X
  3. You get one point when you win
